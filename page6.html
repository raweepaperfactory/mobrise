Delivery

Shipment 

We can deliver to your shipping agent or we can help to find the best shipping agent Shipping cost is paid by buyer. We will make every attempt to ship out your order as soon as possible. Orders placed Monday through Saturday by 2:00pm Central Time will generally leave our warehouse by the next business day. Orders placed after 2:00 p.m. CST or on Sunday and holidays will generally leave our warehouse within 2 business days. Once your product(s) has been shipped, you will receive a shipping confirmation email as well as a shipment tracking number for your order. Shipping is currently available to all of these countries. However, to prevent fraud we reserve the right at our sole discretion to refuse shipping any order, for any reason. 

Description and Price of Goods 

The description and price of the products you order will be as shown on our website at the time you place your order. Every effort is made to ensure that prices shown on our website are accurate at the time you place your order although prices are subject to change without notice. If after acceptance of the order we discover that the goods will be unavailable for longer than 28 days we will inform you and offer you the option of receiving the rest of your order and supplying the unavailable product when it becomes available, or for you to wait for the complete order to be dispatched at a later date. 

Stocks Levels 

RAWEE PAPER FACTORY updates the availability of the entire stock list several times a day. Items that are 'Out Of Stock' will be displayed as such. In the rare event of a problem with availability of a product marked 'In Stock', we will contact you. You may then wish to cancel the order or wait until the item becomes available.
